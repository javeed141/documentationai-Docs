## Overview

YouTube is a large-scale video streaming platform owned by Google, used by more than 2 billion users to upload videos, watch videos, share videos, and stream videos globally.

## What you can do

- Upload videos to share with a global audience.
- Watch videos across a wide range of topics.
- Share videos with others.
- Stream video content globally.
